Health Tips

Practical advice for staying healthy in our community

Proper Handwashing

Wash hands regularly with soap and clean water for at least 20 seconds, especially before eating and after using the toilet. This is one of the most effective ways to prevent diseases.

Clean Environment

Practice proper waste disposal and eliminate stagnant water in your surroundings. Clean homes and streets help prevent dengue, diarrhea, and other diseases.

Healthy Diet

Eat a balanced diet with vegetables, fruits, and adequate protein. Avoid processed foods high in salt, sugar, and unhealthy fats to prevent lifestyle diseases.

Regular Exercise

Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity daily. Simple activities like walking, dancing, or household chores can improve your health.
